Song
Surah 3 (Al-Imran: Noor-e-Imaan)
Lyrics
[Verse 1]
Dil ki raahon mein roshni teri aayat ki hai,
Har safar mein humko tera saath mila hai.
Qur’an ke gehre paaniyon se nikal kar,
Imaan ki kashti ko lehraana sikha hai.
[Chorus]
Ek raah, ek dua, Al-Imran ki yeh sada,
Mohabbat ke rang mein rang jaaye saara jahan.
Tawakkul ka hai yeh geet, dilon ko kare preet,
Khuda ki rahmaton ka barsaata yeh sama!
[Verse 2]
Sabr ki dhoop mein, shukr ki chhaon rakhna,
Haq ki baat pe jhukna, na jhukna jhooth se.
Maryam ki mithaas, Isa ki muhabbat,
Yeh kahaniyan humko deti hain rooh ko roshni!
[Bridge]
Kya humne samjha? Kya humne jaana?
Zindagi ki kitaab ko khud se milaana…
Har lafz mein hai woh noor, har saans mein uski mohabbat,
Al-Imran yeh sikhaaye, rab se hai yeh rishta!
[Outro]
Chalo phir se yeh iraada, imaan ko banaye geet,
Har surah ki roshni se jagmagayenge hum rehmat se…
Noor-e-Imaan ka safar, yeh toh bas pehla kadam hai,
Khuda ke raaste mein, dilon ka yeh milan hai…
