# Raw Content: Surah 3 (Al-Imran: Noor-e-Imaan)


<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-song">Song</h2>



<p>Surah 3 (Al-Imran: Noor-e-Imaan)</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-lyrics">Lyrics</h2>



<p>[Verse 1]<br>Dil ki raahon mein roshni teri aayat ki hai,<br>Har safar mein humko tera saath mila hai.<br>Qur’an ke gehre paaniyon se nikal kar,<br>Imaan ki kashti ko lehraana sikha hai.</p>



<p>[Chorus]<br>Ek raah, ek dua, Al-Imran ki yeh sada,<br>Mohabbat ke rang mein rang jaaye saara jahan.<br>Tawakkul ka hai yeh geet, dilon ko kare preet,<br>Khuda ki rahmaton ka barsaata yeh sama!</p>



<p>[Verse 2]<br>Sabr ki dhoop mein, shukr ki chhaon rakhna,<br>Haq ki baat pe jhukna, na jhukna jhooth se.<br>Maryam ki mithaas, Isa ki muhabbat,<br>Yeh kahaniyan humko deti hain rooh ko roshni!</p>



<p>[Bridge]<br>Kya humne samjha? Kya humne jaana?<br>Zindagi ki kitaab ko khud se milaana…<br>Har lafz mein hai woh noor, har saans mein uski mohabbat,<br>Al-Imran yeh sikhaaye, rab se hai yeh rishta!</p>



<p>[Outro]<br>Chalo phir se yeh iraada, imaan ko banaye geet,<br>Har surah ki roshni se jagmagayenge hum rehmat se…<br>Noor-e-Imaan ka safar, yeh toh bas pehla kadam hai,<br>Khuda ke raaste mein, dilon ka yeh milan hai…</p>
